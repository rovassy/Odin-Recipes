Best Scrambled Eggs Recipe Ever

Description

Proteins like never before, get yours too now

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat pan
  2. Add half of butter cubes and let melt
  3. Add eggs and start to stir with wooden spoon
  4. As eggs cook add the other half of butter cubes
  5. Serve immediately
