Sauce so heavenly you never saw it coming

Description

Proteins like never before, get yours too now

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla pod
  • Butter
  • sugar

Steps

  1. Heat milk in pan
  2. Add Vanilla and let it come up to a boil
  3. Cool the milk and add eggs stir continuosly
  4. Take it off heat and stir in butter 1 cube at a time
  5. Serve whenever
